TJ Webb is back with his first album of 2022, Worms. Worms is a punk rock record, 9 loud songs (plus a couple of tame ones) clocking in at 18 minutes from start to finish. The songs are short, sometimes silly, stripped down, often silly, fast, and mostly silly.

It's the Bloodhound Gang on the tea cups at Disney.

It's Gary Busey at a table read of a Seth MacFarlane script.

It's blink-182 cold cranking a chainsaw.

It's the Presidents of The United States of America meets someone who should be speaking with a professional but is not currently speaking with a professional.
